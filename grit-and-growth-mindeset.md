## Question 3
### What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?
Internal Locus of Control means believing that our success and failure depend on our own actions and decisions, not on luck or other people.

**Key Point:**
The video says that taking responsibility for our actions helps us stay motivated and improve ourselves.
