## Question 2
### How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P.
B = MAP means a behavior happens when Motivation, Ability, and Prompt work together.

* **M = Motivation** → your desire to do something
* **A = Ability** → how easy or simple the habit is
* **P = Prompt** → a reminder or trigger to do the habit
